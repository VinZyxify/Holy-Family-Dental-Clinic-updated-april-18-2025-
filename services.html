Our Dental Services

Oral Prophylaxis

Oral prophylaxis is a professional dental cleaning performed to remove dental plaque and tartar that have accumulated on the teeth over time. This procedure is essential for preventing periodontal disease and maintaining optimal oral health.

What to Expect

During your oral prophylaxis appointment, our dentist will:

  • Examine your teeth and gums for any signs of disease
  • Use specialized instruments to remove plaque and tartar (scaling)
  • Polish your teeth to remove surface stains
  • Apply fluoride treatment if needed
  • Provide personalized oral hygiene instructions

Benefits

Prevents gum disease and tooth decay
Removes stubborn stains for a brighter smile
Helps detect dental problems early
Freshens breath by removing bacteria

Topical Fluoride Treatment

Topical fluoride is a preventive treatment applied to the tooth surface to strengthen enamel and make teeth more resistant to decay. It's particularly beneficial for children but can help patients of all ages maintain strong, healthy teeth.

Treatment Process

Our fluoride treatment is quick and painless:

  • Teeth are cleaned and dried
  • Fluoride gel, foam, or varnish is applied to the teeth
  • Treatment is left on for a few minutes
  • You'll be asked to avoid eating or drinking for 30 minutes after

Who Needs Fluoride Treatment?

Children during their cavity-prone years
Patients with a history of frequent cavities
Those with orthodontic appliances
People with dry mouth conditions

When Is Pit and Fissure Sealants needed?

Your dentist may recommend deep scaling if you have:

  • Gums that bleed easily
  • Persistent bad breath
  • Receding gums or deep periodontal pockets
  • Tartar buildup below the gumline
  • Early signs of bone loss

What to Expect After Treatment

Some tooth sensitivity is normal for a few days
Gums may be tender but should heal within a week
Improved gum health within 2-3 weeks
Regular follow-ups may be needed for maintenance
